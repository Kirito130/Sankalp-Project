Who are we ?

Our journey of Sister concern security firm had its roots laid down in the year 2014 .Taking inspirations from it we are pleased to announce the launch of our new firm “Sankalp Securities and Facilities (SSF)” on the auspicious day of Makar Sankranti 14th Jan 2022. This marked the beginning of new ear in the sector of security services agency. We have always kept our faith in the creation of a team, which is both professional as well as etiquette oriented. What separates SSF from its competitors is our Leadership Team’s dedication to understanding and catering to individual clients’ security needs, our Mission and Vision, and our responsiveness.

What do we do ?

We provide services such as Industrial Security, Commercial Security, Hospital security, House keeping and Event security and so on.


The difference is in the details:

• Managers vetted for qualifications and experience ensure consistent delivery of service that meets and exceeds contracted specifications


The Leadership Team provides:

• Staff guidance and supervision from the classroom to site assignments
• Routine performance reviews at each staff level to ensure superior service commensurate with established company policy and philosophy
