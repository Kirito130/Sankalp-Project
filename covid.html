COVID-19 Safety Measures

Why are Sankalp's security guards essential during the COVID-19 pandemic?

Retail establishments, healthcare facilities, corporate business parks, restaurants and many other businesses are dealing with different security concerns than in the past due to the pandemic. Many business owners and staff members struggle to properly enforce the social distancing procedures while maintaining the daily operations of their businesses. Some businesses have experienced outbursts and violence from irate customers who refuse to follow safety protocols. Still others have closed their doors and worry about looters, thieves and vandals threatening their vacant properties.

In the current state of the country, Sankalp's professionally trained security guards are the best way to deter crime and ensure that your business remains vigilant about enforcing health and safety protocols. Sankalp's guards have specialized knowledge and skills that enable them to identify suspicious behavior, interact appropriately with the public and enforce rules in a fair yet firm manner. To be even more effective, security guards should be aware of advanced recommendations for managing the virus while working for a client and with members of the public which is one of the key features we provide in these times of pandemic.

We Maintain all basic health and safety protocols.

To minimize your risk and reduce the chances of spreading the virus to others, Sankalp's security guards take fundamental precautions such as maintaining a distance of six feet from other people as much as possible, washing hands frequently, using hands and surface sanitizers and avoiding touching the face.

In addition to taking these precautions themselves, Sankalp's security guards enforce these and any other basic protocols on behalf of their clients. The reality is that security guards have to engage with the public in order to fulfill their functions. But taking these necessary precautions is just one way of preventing the spread of the virus and keeping you safe that we provide.

We Use Personal Protection Equipment(PPE) consistently.

If you’ve traveled in an airplane, you know that in the event of an emergency you should secure your oxygen mask before assisting others with their masks. The same concept applies to COVID-19 protection when dealing with the public. While on duty, Sankalp's guards wear the necessary PPE such as masks and gloves. Protecting yourself will allow you to better protect others is what we belive in at Sankalp and to protect you and keep you safe we take every precaution needed to get through these hard times.

Recognize the symptoms of COVID-19.

Security guards have ample interaction with the public. During the pandemic, Sankalp's security guards take the time to learn the signs and symptoms of the virus. Signs of the coronavirus can include cough, body aches, sore throat, difficulty breathing and fever. By being able to identify a potentially infected person, our security guards can take the necessary precautions, deny them access to the premises or report the risk to their clients.

Report exposure and take the appropriate measures.

As with any safety or security threat, Sankalp's guards understand the proper channels for reporting potential exposure to COVID-19. They document the incident and the response procedures taken and carefully report the incident and thus can help to stop the spread of the virus before it gets out of control. It also helps our client be proactive about communicating the potential exposure to their customers and staff members.

Security guards have been especially essential during the coronavirus pandemic. While they continue to do their jobs, guards must be extra vigilant about following health and safety protocols. By following the suggestions above, our security guards significantly reduce their personal risk and the risk to others.
